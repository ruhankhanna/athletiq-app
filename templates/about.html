Built off the experience of real runners, athletIQ Ratings are universal,
no matter your age, gender, or ability.


Every time a runner sets is rated using a fine-tuned logarithmic
function, for each event. A final rating is determined
by calculating a weighted average of a runner's times in the last year, per event.

Our Vision for the Future

At athletIQ, we imagine a world where every runner — from beginners to professionals — is part of a unified, intelligent running ecosystem.

We aim to become the go-to platform for athletes to:

  • Compete in ability-based races — not just age-grouped
  • Host and manage meets from anywhere
  • Find clubs and run groups instantly
  • Track trends and scout athletes with powerful analytics

With the support of Microsoft and AWS, and inspired by models like UTR, we're building the future of running powered by data.

Stay tuned for more updates, including athletIQ Premium — our upcoming subscription service that will offer advanced AI-driven analytics and early access to new features.
