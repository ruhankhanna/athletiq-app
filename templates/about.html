About athletIQ

athletIQ isn’t just another stats site — it’s the future of recruiting intelligence.

We started as a community project, but quickly realized the real need was giving college track & XC programs the tools to recruit with data, not gut feel.

We pull verified race results, then layer on analytics to give coaches the most accurate picture of a runner's performance:

• Recency-weighted performance so you see an athlete’s current shape, not just their lifetime PR.
• Trajectory tracking to capture who’s trending up (or plateauing).
• 3/6/12-month forecasts that give you realistic, Machine Learning-based projections,
• Meet-day normalization to level out altitude, weather, and “fast course” effects.

Our mission: make recruiting faster, fairer, and more data-driven. With athletIQ, every program can scout talent with the clarity and confidence of a pro analytics department.

Our Mission

Build a reliable, data-driven foundation for collegiate running so every program can identify talent more effectively — and every athlete can be evaluated more fairly.

What We’re Building

  • Performance projections at 3, 6, and 12 months
  • Recency-weighted analytics that reflect current shape
  • Meet/course normalization for fair comparisons
  • Nationwide scouting views tailored for coaching staffs

Powered by modern cloud infrastructure and inspired by the best rating systems in sport, athletIQ is purpose-built for the needs of college coaches.
