About athletIQ

athletIQ started as a community ratings project and has evolved into a platform focused on helping college track & cross-country programs recruit smarter. We combine verified results from Athletic.net with statistical modeling to give coaches a clearer, fairer view of athlete performance.

Instead of simple PR lists, we highlight what matters for recruiting: recency-weighted performance, short-term trajectory, realistic 3/6/12-month forecasts, and meet-day bias adjustments that make comparisons more fair across courses and conditions.

Our Mission

Build a reliable, data-driven foundation for collegiate running so every program can identify talent more effectively — and every athlete can be evaluated more fairly.

What We’re Building

  • Performance projections at 3, 6, and 12 months
  • Recency-weighted analytics that reflect current shape
  • Meet/course normalization for fair comparisons
  • Nationwide scouting views tailored for coaching staffs

Powered by modern cloud infrastructure and inspired by the best rating systems in sport, athletIQ is purpose-built for the needs of college coaches.
